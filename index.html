Nikah Ceremony

26 December 2026 · Delhi · The sacred marriage contract

🕌 Laraib Siddiqui weds Musharraf Jahan
✅

Laraib's Nikah Checklist

0 / 7 done
  • Book a Qazi — contact local mosque or Delhi Waqf Board
  • Agree on Mahr amount with Musharraf and both families
  • Confirm 2 adult Muslim male witnesses (Shahidain)
  • Carry a pen for signing the Nikah Nama
  • Carry dates to distribute after the Nikah (Sunnah)
  • Carry original Aadhaar card and photo ID
  • Prepare heartfelt words for Musharraf after the Nikah
🔑

Islamic Requirements

RequirementDetails
QaziQualified Islamic scholar to officiate
Shahidain2 adult Muslim male witnesses
MahrObligatory gift from Laraib to Musharraf — belongs entirely to her
WaliMusharraf's guardian (father/male relative) must be present
Ijab & QuboolVerbal offer and acceptance in one sitting
Nikah NamaWritten contract — signed by all parties
📋

Ceremony Order

  1. Laraib seated with witnesses and Qazi
  2. Recitation of Khutbah-tun-Nikah (Islamic marriage sermon)
  3. Recitation of Surah Al-Fatiha, Al-Baqarah (2:187), An-Nisa (4:1), Al-Ahzab (33:70–71)
  4. Qazi asks Laraib: Ijab — "Do you accept this Nikah?"
  5. Musharraf accepts through her Wakil: Qubool — said 3 times
  6. Mahr formally announced and agreed
  7. Nikah Nama signed by Laraib, Wakil, and both witnesses
  8. Dua for the couple by the Qazi
  9. Distribution of dates (Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ)

Honeymoon · 11 Days · Dec 28 – Jan 7

Vietnam Honeymoon 🇻🇳

Laraib & Musharraf · Delhi → Hanoi → Ha Long Bay → Hoi An → Ho Chi Minh City → Delhi

✈️ Book flights NOW — Dec 28 is peak season
🎫

Q1: Book Now vs. Flying on 28 Dec?

Short answer: Book NOW. Dec 28 falls in the Christmas + New Year global peak window. Prices double or triple if you wait.
When you bookEst. fare (return/person)Risk
Now (July 2026)₹25,000 – ₹35,000✅ Low — good seats
Sep–Oct 2026₹35,000 – ₹50,000⚠️ Medium
Nov–Dec 2026₹55,000 – ₹85,000+❌ High — may sell out

Best airlines: IndiGo/AirAsia via KL or Bangkok · Vietnam Airlines · Thai Airways via BKK
Tip: Book open jaw — fly INTO Hanoi (HAN), fly OUT of Ho Chi Minh City (SGN). No backtracking needed.
Where to book: Skyscanner · Google Flights · MakeMyTrip

🏠

Q2: Airbnb vs. Hotel?

Verdict: Use Airbnb for most cities, cruise cabin for Ha Long Bay.

Ha Long BayCruise cabin (Airbnb not applicable)₹8,000 – ₹25,000

Verdict: Self-plan. A package adds 15–25% cost, won't allow Airbnb, and ignores your halal food needs. Plan it yourself — it's easier than you think.

Hanoi — Days 1–2

Day 1 — Old Quarter & Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Explore Hanoi Old Quarter's 36 narrow streets
  • Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple — peaceful, romantic
  • Temple of Literature (1070 AD — stunning)
  • Rooftop dinner overlooking the lake at dusk
Day 2 — Culture & Architecture
  • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum (exterior) and Ba Dinh Square
  • Vietnam Museum of Ethnology
  • Tran Quoc Pagoda — stunning lakeside pagoda for golden hour photos
  • Evening: Bia Hoi corner walk and street food exploration
🛳️

Ha Long Bay — Days 3–4 · Most Romantic Part

⭐ Ha Long Bay cruise is the crown jewel of this trip. Book as early as possible — Dec slots sell out fast.
Day 3 — Board the Cruise
  • Private car transfer from Hanoi (3.5 hrs)
  • Board cruise at noon · kayaking through limestone karsts
  • Sunset on deck with cocktails
  • 3-course dinner on the boat (inform them of halal requirement)
Day 4 — Sunrise & Return
  • Dawn sunrise on deck — the most romantic moment of the trip
  • Morning swim · floating village visit
  • Tai chi on the sundeck (try it together!)
  • Disembark afternoon · evening flight to Da Nang
