💍 Mangni (Engagement)

Formal announcement of commitment · Ring exchange · Both families together

📋 Ceremony Order

  1. Welcome and seating of both families
  2. Recitation of Surah Al-Fatiha and dua for barakah
  3. Exchange of rings (groom places ring on bride's finger)
  4. Sweets / mithai distributed to guests
  5. Family photographs
  6. Dinner / refreshments

✅ Groom's Checklist

  • Select the engagement ring (consult bride's preference)
  • Confirm Mangni date with both families
  • Choose and purchase Mangni outfit
  • Prepare a short speech or dua for the ceremony
  • Coordinate gifts or mithai to bring from groom's side

🕌 Nikah Ceremony

The sacred Islamic marriage contract · The most important function

"Our Lord, grant us from among our wives and offspring comfort to our eyes and make us an example for the righteous." — Surah Al-Furqan (25:74)

🔑 Islamic Requirements

RequirementDetailsStatus
QaziQualified Islamic scholar to officiateTo Book
2 Witnesses2 adult Muslim male witnesses (Shahidain)To Confirm
MahrObligatory gift/dowry from groom to brideTo Agree
WaliBride's guardian must be present or consentTo Confirm
Ijab & QuboolVerbal offer and acceptance in one sittingSunnah
Nikah NamaWritten contract signed by all partiesQazi Prepares

📋 Ceremony Order

  1. Groom seated with witnesses and Qazi
  2. Recitation of Khutbah-tun-Nikah (Islamic marriage sermon)
  3. Recitation of Surah Al-Fatiha, Al-Baqarah (2:187), An-Nisa (4:1), Al-Ahzab (33:70–71)
  4. Qazi asks groom: Ijab — offer of marriage
  5. Bride accepts through her Wakil: Qubool — said 3 times
  6. Mahr formally announced and agreed
  7. Nikah Nama signed by groom, Wakil, and witnesses
  8. Dua for the couple by the Qazi
  9. Distribution of dates (Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ)
  10. Congratulations from family and guests

✅ Groom's Checklist

  • Book a Qazi — contact local mosque or Delhi Waqf Board
  • Confirm Mahr amount with bride and both families
  • Confirm 2 adult Muslim male witnesses
  • Bring a pen for signing the Nikah Nama
  • Carry dates to distribute after the Nikah
  • Carry original Aadhaar card for documentation
  • Prepare heartfelt words for your bride after the Nikah

🤲 Dua for the Groom

"Allahumma inni as'aluka khayraha wa khayra ma jabaltaha alayhi, wa a'udhu bika min sharriha wa sharri ma jabaltaha alayh"
O Allah, I ask You for the good in her and the good of her nature, and I seek refuge in You from the evil in her and the evil of her nature.

🍽️ Walima Reception

Sunnah feast · Celebration with community · Within 3 days of Nikah

"Hold a Walima, even if it is with a single sheep." — Sahih Bukhari

✅ Groom's Checklist

  • Confirm Walima date — within 3 days of Nikah
  • Confirm venue for Walima
  • Choose Walima outfit (suit or elegant kurta)
  • Plan couple's entry — nasheed or simple walk-in

⚖️ Legal Tasks & Emergency Kit

Nikah Nama · Civil registration · Day-of vendor contacts · Emergency bag

📜 Legal Checklist

  • Collect the original signed Nikah Nama from the Qazi
  • Make 2–3 photocopies of Nikah Nama — store safely
  • Visit SDM office for civil marriage certificate (Special Marriage Act)
  • Update nominees on bank accounts and insurance policies
  • Add spouse to health insurance policy
