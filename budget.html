💡 Ways to Cut ~₹5–7L
🍽️ Reduce Walima guest list

Going from 1,000 to 600 guests saves ₹2.5–3L on food alone. Biggest single lever.

Save ₹2.5–3L
🏛️ Negotiate venue package

Bundle Nikah + Walima at the same venue (Dabur) for a package deal. Ask for 15–20% off for booking both days.

Save ₹40–60k
👗 Simplify groom clothes

₹1.5L for personal clothes is high. A good sherwani for Nikah + simple kurta for other events keeps it under ₹80k.

Save ₹60–70k
📷 Book early / bundle photographer

Book 3–4 months out and negotiate 1 photographer covering all 4 events for ₹75k instead of ₹1L.

Save ₹25k
🪙 Fewer gold grams

15g instead of 25g at 22K saves ~₹78k while still being a meaningful mahr / gift.

Save ₹78k
🎵 Skip band / opt for DJ

A DJ at ₹25–30k gives the same energy as a ₹1L band for Walima.

Save ₹70–75k
